# Realm Craft — Vision Document

> **Build Anything. Drive Everything. Fight Anywhere. Create Forever.**

## Vision

Realm Craft is a next-generation AAA sandbox platform that combines the best ideas from
open-world driving, creative sandbox building, competitive multiplayer, survival, RPG
progression, and user-generated experiences into one original game.

Realm Craft is not just a game — it is a platform where players can build worlds, race,
battle, roleplay, survive, create games, host events, and explore an ever-expanding
universe. The goal is to become **the definitive sandbox platform for the next decade**.

## Design Philosophy

- Freedom
- Creativity
- Realism where appropriate
- Fast gameplay
- Infinite replayability
- Community creation
- High performance
- AAA presentation
- Massive multiplayer
- Endless progression

Every mechanic should interconnect so the world feels alive and player-driven.

---

## Core Gameplay Pillars

### 🚗 Open-World Driving

Advanced vehicle physics with hundreds of drivable vehicles: hypercars, sports cars,
off-road vehicles, trucks, motorcycles, boats, aircraft, helicopters, trains, and
construction equipment.

- Vehicle tuning and full customization
- Dynamic traffic and AI traffic simulation
- Racing championships, drift events, rally events
- Highway cruising and driving schools

### 🧱 Creative Sandbox

Players can build almost anything: houses, cities, entire countries, theme parks,
castles, spaceships, factories, roller coasters, schools, businesses, skyscrapers,
hidden bases, underground bunkers, islands, and floating cities.

**Building tools:** terrain editing, sculpting, painting, roads, rivers, water editing,
weather editing, lighting editor, material editor, logic systems, wiring, NPC creator,
quest creator, cutscene editor, animation tools.

### ⚔ Combat

Fast, responsive, and competitive. First person and third person. PvP, PvE, ranked,
casual, and custom servers.

**Weapons:** pistols, rifles, SMGs, shotguns, snipers, LMGs, rocket launchers, energy
weapons, melee weapons, shields, throwable gadgets.

**Movement:** sprint, slide, vault, mantle, wall run, wall jump, grappling hook,
zipline, wingsuit, swimming, climbing, dive mechanics.

### 🌎 Massive Living World

One seamless map — everything explorable, no loading screens.

Modern cities, villages, forests, mountains, snow biomes, deserts, jungles, volcanoes,
beaches, oceans, rivers, lakes, caves, underground cities, mines, highways, airports,
a space center, military bases, race circuits, hidden ruins, islands, and sky islands.

---

## Dynamic World

Living ecosystems: wildlife, NPC traffic, pedestrians, dynamic economy, police, fire
department, ambulances, construction, and natural disasters (tornadoes, hurricanes,
floods, wildfires, earthquakes).

**Fully simulated weather:** rain, thunderstorms, snow, blizzards, fog, sandstorms,
heat waves, dynamic clouds, dynamic seasons. Weather affects driving, combat, AI,
visibility, terrain, and vehicles.

## Vehicles

Hundreds at launch: hypercars, supercars, muscle cars, electric vehicles, SUVs, pickup
trucks, rally cars, formula cars, motorcycles, boats, jetskis, helicopters, airplanes,
cargo planes, tanks, utility vehicles, trains.

Every vehicle includes full interiors, working dashboards, real lighting, working
mirrors, opening doors/hood/trunk, damage simulation, and dirt/mud/snow/water
accumulation.

**Simulation-quality physics:** weight transfer, tire pressure, tire deformation,
suspension simulation, aerodynamics, damage, fuel, engine temperature, turbo systems,
differential simulation, AWD/FWD/RWD, electric powertrains, hybrid systems.

## Character Progression

Players level up through every activity: driving, combat, building, crafting,
exploration, trading, parkour, flying, fishing, survival.

**Unlocks:** skills, cosmetics, vehicles, blueprints, building tools, AI companions,
pets, titles, emotes.

## Survival Mode (Optional)

Hunger, thirst, temperature, crafting, farming, mining, fishing, cooking, shelter,
electricity, power grids, resource gathering.

## Multiplayer

- 200+ players per server, dedicated servers, cross-platform
- Voice chat, text chat, guilds, friends, parties
- Trading, marketplace, spectator mode, replay mode, custom game modes

## Creator Platform

Players can build racing games, battle arenas, adventure games, story campaigns, RPGs,
obstacle courses, survival servers, city roleplay, sports games, minigames, and
educational worlds.

**Built-in tools:** visual scripting, optional C# scripting, AI-assisted world
generation, terrain editor, asset editor, animation editor, UI editor, sound editor,
multiplayer testing, performance profiler.

## Economy

Player-driven: trading, marketplace, vehicle dealerships, property ownership,
businesses, rental systems, auction house, creator marketplace, cosmetic shop.
Creators can monetize approved experiences while preserving fair gameplay.

## AI Systems

NPC citizens, dynamic traffic, enemy behaviors, driving instructors, AI teammates,
procedural missions, adaptive difficulty, AI-generated events, smart wildlife,
intelligent police.

## Audio

Spatial audio, realistic engine recordings, environmental ambience, dynamic music,
interactive soundtrack, voice reactions, weather audio, city ambience.

## Photo & Replay Mode

Manual camera, drone camera, director mode, slow motion, keyframe animation, color
grading, filters, 8K screenshots, replay editor.

## Accessibility

Full controller support, keyboard & mouse, custom keybinds, colorblind modes, subtitle
customization, UI scaling, text-to-speech, speech-to-text, motion reduction, aim
assistance options.

## Technical Targets

- Seamless open-world streaming
- Dedicated multiplayer servers
- Advanced LOD and occlusion systems
- Multithreaded simulation
- High-performance networking
- Modular architecture
- Extensive mod support
- Anti-cheat for competitive modes
- Cloud saves and cross-progression
- Scalable graphics settings (low-end to high-end PCs)

## Long-Term Vision

Realm Craft should feel less like a single game and more like a living digital
universe. Players should be able to spend years creating, competing, exploring,
socializing, and building new experiences. Every update should expand the platform
with new regions, mechanics, creator tools, and community events, allowing the game
to evolve continuously while maintaining a cohesive, high-quality experience.
